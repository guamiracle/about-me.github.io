Miracle

In line with the magic that surrounds the pen name, the word "Miracle" itself carries a deep meaning.
Miracle, in all its splendor, refers to an event or occurrence that is extraordinary, unexpected, and often considered a mixture of luck and impossibility.
In each of the characters that make up the word, there is a message that miracles can arise amidst the limitations and small surprises of life, turning the world into a stage full of unexpected beauty.
Miracle is not just a word, but a call to look at life with the eyes of an open heart, and grasp each moment with hope for the miracles that can happen.
